Age Limit

There is no age limit for appearing in JEE Main 2021.

Candidates who have passed class 12th / equivalent examination in 2019, 2020 or appearing in 2021 irrespective of their age can appear in JEE Main 2021.

Number of Attempts

JEE Main candidates can participate in the entrance exam for 3 consecutive years.

The exam will be conducted twice a year, candidates may appear in both the sessions.

If a candidate sits for both the sessions, it shall be counted as a single attempt.

Educational Qualification

For Paper 1 (B.Tech), the candidates must have studied Physics and Mathematics as the compulsory subjects in Class 12th, and Chemistry/ Biotechnology can be optional subjects.

Physics:For the last 5 years, Physics has been the toughest section of all which includes a lot of lengthy questions. The numerical part of the paper is generally asked from 11th standard whereas the conceptual part is mostly from 12th standard. One must concentrate more on the latter as it is a comparatively scoring part.

With a 70% accuracy, if a candidate attempts 15 questions of Physics section, he/she can score more than 30 marks.

The Chemistry: section is believed to be the easiest one where organic and inorganic questions are more of conceptual type. These questions should be given more focus due to direct correspondence with respective options.

If a candidate is able to attempt 22 Chemistry questions with 80% accuracy, he/she can score more than 60 marks.
