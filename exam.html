How to Apply Online?

Candidates will be able to access the JEE Advanced application form 2021 only in online mode, however, payment of the application fee can be done in online or offline mode. The JEE Advanced application form 2021 will be made available on the official website. Candidates will also have to select their preferred choice of JEE Advanced exam centres. Students may check the JEE Advanced eligibility criteria released by IIT Kharagpur.

How to Fill JEE Main Application Form 2021?

Document Uploading

Step 3 -

Payment of Registration Fee

Detailed procedure to fill JEE Main Application Form 2021

Step1-

Fill JEE Advanced Registration Form:

Firstly, candidates will have to log into the JEE Advanced application portal using the JEE Main 2021 login id and password. Upon successful login, all personal, academic and contact details as furnished in the JEE Main application form will be displayed on the screen.

Candidates will be given an option to provide an additional phone number and email id. Students will also have to enter some other details for the JEE Advanced 2021 exam such as choice of examination cities (08 choices), class XII (or equivalent) examination Roll no. and much more.

Step2-

Uploading the document:In the next step, candidates will be required to upload the documents specified by the concerned authorities. Note that all the documents should be uploaded as per the specifications.

Step3-

Payment of JEE Advanced 2021 Application Fee In the next step, candidates will be required to upload the documents specified by the concerned authorities. Note that all the documents should be uploaded as per the specifications.

JEE Main May 2021 Application Form (Soon)

JEE Main May 2021 application form is expected to be released anytime soon in June. The candidates who have not registered earlier can complete the application process for the next phase. This year, almost 22 lakh students are going to appear for JEE Main (all phases combined). Candidates belonging to reserved categories such as EWS/ SC/ ST/ OBC NCL will have to upload the Category Certificate while filling the form. If the candidates can't upload the same, he/ she will have to sign an undertaking and submit the same by May 2021. The exam will be conducted in four phases, i.e., February, March, April, and May.

Eligible to Fill JEE Main Application

It is imperative to check the JEE Main Eligibility criteria before starting JEE Main Registration since your candidature will be canceled if, at any stage, you’re found ineligible,

Age Limit

There is no age limit for appearing in JEE Main 2021.

Candidates who have passed class 12th / equivalent examination in 2019, 2020 or appearing in 2021 irrespective of their age can appear in JEE Main 2021.

Number of Attempts

JEE Main candidates can participate in the entrance exam for 3 consecutive years.

The exam will be conducted twice a year, candidates may appear in both the sessions.

If a candidate sits for both the sessions, it shall be counted as a single attempt.

Educational Qualification

For Paper 1 (B.Tech), the candidates must have studied Physics and Mathematics as the compulsory subjects in Class 12th, and Chemistry/ Biotechnology can be optional subjects.

For Paper 1 (B.Tech), the candidates must have studied Physics and Mathematics as the compulsory subjects in Class 12th, and Chemistry/ Biotechnology can be optional subjects.

Documents required for JEE Main

Class 10th and 12th academic details

Aadhaar Card or Aadhaar Enrollment Number

Scanned images of passport, voter ID or any valid Govt ID

JEE Main Application Form Correction 2021

JEE Main Form Correction is a one-time facility for candidates to make changes in the details of the application form.

Depending on the changes made, if additional fee payment is required, the final changes will be reflected after the payment is made.

Candidates must go through the changes as no further changes shall be allowed in the application form.

Also, NTA has opened an image correction window which is exclusively for rectifying any errors in uploading of pictures.
