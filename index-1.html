What is B Tech (Bachelor of Technology)?

Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree programme awarded to candidates after the completion of four years of study in the field. Engineering is one of the most popular courses in India and there are many institutes that offer the course to aspiring students. For admissions, the most common BTech entrance examination is JEE Main and JEE Advanced. Along with these national level entrance examinations, there are many state and private level examinations that the students can attempt for admissions. The basic eligibility criteria for BTech is 10+2th standard with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics, however, there are additional criteria in every entrance exam and institute.

Eligibility Criteria for BE/BTech

The eligibility criteria to participate in the campus placements is mentioned below:

Min Qualification Required: 10+2

Min. Marks Required (Aggregate): 50% to 70% varies from college to college

Age Limit: Some colleges and entrance may have an age bar for aspirants who seek admission in B.Tech courses. Candidates should not be more than 24 years of age counted from the date of the examination

B.Tech Entrance Exams

JEE Main: Conduction by National Testing Agency, JEE Main is the entrance examination for BTech admissions into NITs, IIITs and GFTIs. JEE Main consists of two papers - Paper 1 (BE/BTech) and Paper 2 (BArch/BPlan)

JEE Advance: It is a national level entrance examination for BTech admissions into IITs. Students have to qualify JEE Main to attempt JEE Advanced.

VITEEE: One of the most popular private institute entrance examination, VITEEE offers admissions into the BTech courses offered by its various campuses.

MHT CET: Through MHT CET, students can apply for BTech admissions into the BTech institutes of the state.
