Criteria & Eligibility

Criteria

Candidates will be required to apply to UPSEE in online mode only, as per the guidelines suggested by the government, on the official website of UPSEE.

Candidates will have to enter in the details truthfully and completely, as the incorrect and incomplete applications may be rejected, thus cancelling your admissions through the exam.

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed their class 12 board examination in the stream of science with compulsory subjects such as Physics and Maths.

They must also ensure that they have obtained an aggregate score of 45% in the qualifying exam. Reserved category candidates will need to have scored an aggregate score of 40% for the same.

Selection

IIT Delhi PG admissions typically begin in the month of March

IIT Delhi BTech admission generally starts from the month of September with JEE Main registrations

Admission to BTech is offered on the basis of candidate's JEE Advanced rank followed by JoSAA counselling

To be eligible for the MSc programme, candidates are required to qualify IIT JAM

To secure admission to MBA, candidates must have a valid CAT score

IIT Delhi MTech admission is based on GATE score and MDes admission is based on CEED scores. IITD also conducts its own written test and/or interview for selection in these courses.

How to Apply

Visit the official website of IIT.

Select the preferred course.

Login to fill-up the form.

Fill up the form with the required details.

Upload the documents carefully.

Submit/save the application form.

Pay the application fee through SBI-Collect.
